Primus

As of March 31, 2011, Primus Networks, Inc. was acquired by White Glove Technologies LLC. Primus Networks, Inc. provides IT support services. It offers email and spam protection, phone, computer support, and help desk services for law firms and legal practices in Austin, Houston, San Antonio, Dallas, and Fort Worth. The company also provides managed desktop, server, network, and security services; business continuity, disaster recovery, and data backup and recovery services; IT consulting services, including strategic planning, hardware and software technical audits, legal audits, and software implementation services, as well as training services on the use of new hardware and software; and Microsoft-based email messaging and office collaboration tools. In addition, it offers hosted mail solutions, including Microsoft exchange, Blackberry enterprise server (BES), smart phone integration, and POP/IMAP mail; software-as-a-service application services, including SharePoint and CITRIX; hosted virtual server and storage area networks; and Website hosting services for Linux and Windows systems. Further, the company sells and services business phone systems. Primus Networks, Inc. was founded in 2000 and is based in Austin, Texas.
