Cogent

Cogent, Inc. offers pumping and process equipment products and services. The company provides pump control, wastewater treatment equipment, water treatment equipment, centrifugal pump, submersible pumps, and vertical and horizontal pumps. Additionally, it offers pump and motor control systems, SCADA systems and instrumentation, pipe plugs, hydrostatic test pumps, and automatic pump controls and dialer systems. The company caters to municipal, industrial, and commercial sectors. Cogent, Inc. is based in Kansas City, Missouri.

Cogent's AS (174) has one of the highest-ranked connectivity degrees on the Internet.
