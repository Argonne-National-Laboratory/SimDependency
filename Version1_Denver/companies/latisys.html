Latisys

Latisys Corp. provides Infrastructure-as-a-Service solutions in the United States and internationally. It offers hybrid cloud hosting services; enterprise cloud services; managed hosting services; data center services, such as data center cooling, data center security, network connectivity, data center locations, and green information technology solutions, as well as co-location services and equipment; disaster recovery services; and managed information technology services. The company also provides managed storage services, such as storage area network, data protection, and storage replication and snapshots; managed performance services, such as network load balancing, information technology performance monitoring and reporting, and database management; managed information technology security services, such as threat management, managed firewalls, managed VPN, OS patch management, managed antivirus, and managed SIEM and vSOC services; and SOC 2 type II and SOC 3, PCI compliant, and HIPAA compliant hosting services. In addition, it offers application hosting services, including Software-as-a-Service, e-commerce, and big data storage and analytics data hosting; customer support services; and Platform-as-a-Service and back office hosting services. The company serves medium-sized businesses, enterprise customers, and government agencies in various industries, such as healthcare, education, legal, logistics, finance, media and marketing, manufacturing, utilities, and non-profit. It has strategic partnerships with HP, Microsoft, EMC Software, Cisco Systems, VMware, Juniper, Redhat, Palo Alto Networks, F5, and Compellent. Latisys Corp. was formerly known as Managed Data Holdings LLC and changed its name to Latisys Corp. in March, 2009. The company was founded in 1994 and is based in Englewood, Colorado with data centers in Ashburn, Virginia; Chicago, Illinois; Denver, Colorado; Irvine, California; and London, United Kingdom. Latisys Corp. operates as a subsidiary of Latisys Holdings, LLC.
