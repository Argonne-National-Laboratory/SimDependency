Raging Wire

RagingWire Data Centers, Inc. provides data center services in the United States. Its data center services include colocation; network services; and professional services, such as project management, cable management, information technology infrastructure design and installation, and relocation services. The company serves enterprise, financial, Internet, private, and public clients. The company was formerly known as RagingWire Enterprise Solutions, Inc. The company was founded in 2000 and is headquartered in Sacramento, California. It has data center colocation facilities in Sacramento, California; and Ashburn, Virginia. As of February 3, 2014, RagingWire Data Centers, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of NTT Communications Corporation.
