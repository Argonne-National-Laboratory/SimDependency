Verizon

Verizon Business Global LLC provides Internet protocol, data, voice, and wireless communication solutions to businesses and federal government agencies. It offers VoIP, data and IP services, Internet access, security, information technology solutions and hosting, managed networks, premises equipment, contact centers, conferencing, and voice products. It also provides customer premise equipment and maintenance, voice and data networking, security and resilience planning, end-to-end management, and contact center solutions. In addition, the company offers application and performance management, collaboration, enterprise mobility, cloud computing, and business continuity services; and global fixed mobile convergence services that reduce mobile phone roaming charges for European workers. The company offers data discovery, identification, and security classification suite of security consulting services for health care in North America, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific region. The company serves channel partners and value added resellers, retail, high-tech, healthcare, federal/state/local government, and education sectors in the United States, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, South Africa, Sweden, and the United Kingdom. It was formerly known as MCI, LLC and changed its name to Verizon Business Global LLC in November 2006. The company was founded in 1968 and is based in Ashburn, Virginia with an additional office in Munich, Germany. As of January 6, 2006, Verizon Business Global LLC operates as a subsidiary of Verizon Communications, Inc.
