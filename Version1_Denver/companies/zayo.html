Zayo

Zayo Group, LLC provides bandwidth infrastructure solutions. It operates through three segments: Physical Infrastructure, Cloud and Connectivity, and Other. The Physical Infrastructure segment provides raw bandwidth infrastructure services, such as dark fiber, mobile infrastructure, and colocation and interconnection services to clients that require more control of their internal networks, as well as leases dark fiber pairs to its customers. Its mobile infrastructure services provide direct fiber connections to cell towers, small cells, hub sites, and mobile switching centers; and datacenters offer colocation and interconnection services to its customers. This segment serves carriers and other communication service providers, Internet service providers, wireless service providers, media and content companies, enterprises, and other companies that have the expertise to run their own fiber optic networks or require interconnected technical space. The Cloud and Connectivity segment provides bandwidth infrastructure solutions over its metro, regional, and long-haul fiber networks. Its services include wavelength, Ethernet, IP, SONET, and cloud services. This segment serves carriers, financial services companies, healthcare, government institutions, education institutions, and other enterprises. The Other segment provides network and technical services to customers in designing, acquiring, and maintaining their networks. The company also offers colocation and infrastructure as a service. As of June 30, 2015, it owned or leased a fiber network of approximately 85,500 fiber route miles or 6,700,000 fiber miles in the United States and Europe. The company was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in Boulder, Colorado. Zayo Group, LLC operates as a subsidiary of Zayo Group Holdings, Inc.
