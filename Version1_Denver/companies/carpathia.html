Carpathia

Carpathia Hosting, Inc., a cloud operator, provides cloud services and managed hosting for enterprises and federal agencies worldwide. The company offers information technology infrastructure and management; cloud computing services, such as private cloud, community cloud, public cloud, and hybrid cloud; managed hosting services, including managed dedicated solutions and managed services; and colocation services. It also provides Continuous Assurance Platform, an automated compliance management solution that helps government and healthcare organizations to organize, collect, and analyze compliance-related data that simplifies the auditing process; and Cloud Operations Platform, a multi-cloud management tool that provides customers with a consolidated portal view to monitor and manage Amazon Web Services cloud resources and dedicated and private cloud resources in Carpathiaâ€™s data centers. The company was founded in 2003 and is based in Dulles, Virginia. As of June 16, 2015, Carpathia Hosting, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Quality Technology Services Holding, LLC.
