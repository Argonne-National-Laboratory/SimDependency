InfoRelay Online Systems

InfoRelay Online Systems, Inc. provides enterprise-level data center and information technology (IT) managed solutions to clients ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies in the United States and internationally. Its services include colocation, network bandwidth, metro Ethernet, dedicated servers, network security, and backup and recovery; and managed services, such as technical support, server monitoring and support, and EPIC support packages. The company serves government contractors, enterprises, healthcare firms, financial institutions, and IT and hosting providers. InfoRelay Online Systems, Inc. was founded in 1995 and is based in Herndon, Virginia. It has data centers in New York, New York; Ashburn, Reston, and Herndon, Virginia; Miami, Florida; Chicago, Illinois; Dallas, Texas; and Los Angeles and San Jose, California.
