365 Data Centers

365 Data Centers Inc. provides colocation services for small and mid-sized businesses, telecom carriers, and cloud service providers. It offers data center colocation, cloud storage, interconnection, remote hands, design, and quick start colocation bundle services. 365 Data Centers Inc. was formerly known as 365 Main Inc. and changed its name to 365 Data Centers Inc. in April 2014. The company was founded in 2002 and is based in Emeryville, California. It has data centers in Buffalo and New York, New York; Chicago, Illinois; Cleveland, Ohio; Dallas, Texas; Detroit, Michigan; Indianapolis, Indiana; Nashville, Tennessee; Philadelphia and Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania; Phoenix, Arizona; Reston, Virginia; St. Louis, Missouri; San Jose, California; Seattle, Washington; and Tampa, Florida.
