NTT

NTT America, Inc. provides network and IT solutions to corporations in North America and internationally. It offers managed private network services, including MPLS IP VPN, private line, and Ethernet; Internet networking services, including IP transit/Internet access, IPv6 and IPv6/IPv4 dual, content delivery, DDos countermeasures, and layer 2 VPN; and IT services, such as security management, IT management, and systems integration. The company also provides data center services that include managed hosting; virtualization and cloud; managed security; and managed data storage, backup, and recovery. NTT America, Inc. has a strategic partnership with Advantage Communications Group. The company was founded in 1987 and is headquartered in New York, New York. It has additional offices in Jersey City, New Jersey; Dallas, Texas; Washington, District of Columbia; Boca Raton, Florida; Chicago, Illinois; Denver, Colorado; San Jose and Los Angeles, California; New York, New York; Vienna and Sterling, Virginia; and Redmond, Washington. NTT America, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of NTT Communications Corporation.
