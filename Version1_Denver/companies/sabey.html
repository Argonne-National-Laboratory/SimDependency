Sabey

Sabey, Inc., a real estate development and investment company, is engaged in the design, construction, and operation of critical and other technical space for the data center, medical and life sciences, education, government, and military sectors. The company designs, builds, owns, and operates multi-tenant data centers in Seattle, Quincy, and Wenatchee, Washington; Manhattan, New York; and Ashburn, Virginia. It also provides wholesale colocation/turnkey modules, site selections, remote hands services, onsite support, construction and development services, and design services. The company was founded in 1971 and is based in Seattle, Washington.
