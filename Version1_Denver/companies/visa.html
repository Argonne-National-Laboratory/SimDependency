Visa

Visa U.S.A. Inc., a payments technology company, operates retail electronic payments network in the United States. It also administers Visa payment programs. The company product platform includes consumer credit, consumer debit and cash access, and prepaid and commercial programs. It offers products and services over a secure payments network to support payment programs offered by its member financial institutions to their consumer, commercial, and merchant customers. Visa U.S.A. Inc. was formerly known as National BankAmericard, Inc. and changed its name to Visa U.S.A. Inc. in January, 1977. The company was founded in 1970 and is headquartered in Foster City, California. Visa U.S.A. Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Visa Inc.
