Verio

Verio Inc. provides online business solutions to SMBs worldwide. Its solutions include cloud, web hosting, managed services, application hosting, and software as a service applications. It offers various hosting plans, including basic Web hosting plans, Microsoft SharePoint hosting, and mobile Websites; Ecommerce hosting; and various hosting platforms, such as Windows hosting, Linux hosting, and UNIX hosting. The company also provides various professional services, including Website marketing services, professional Website design packages and services, Website management services, professional security services, and mobile Website development. In addition, it offers business infrastructure solutions, small business growth solutions, communication solutions, collaboration solutions, online presence solutions, and security solutions. The company distributes its solutions through its network of OEM and channel partners. Verio Inc. was formerly known as WorldNet Access, Inc. and changed its name to Verio Inc. in April 1997. The company was incorporated in 1996 and is based in Orem, Utah with data centers in Dulles and Sterling, Virginia; and San Jose, California. As of May 26, 2015, Verio Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Endurance International Group Holdings, Inc.
